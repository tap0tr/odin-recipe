Recipe Description

This is one the simplest drink desserts that I enjoy, especially on rainy days when I'm stuck at home.

I remember back in high school, I would walk myself to the nearest diner and order myself a cup of Coke Float almost everyday.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream or any flavor
  • 1 litre Coke
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Take a spoonful of ice cream into a cup. Get as much ice cream as you want but don't fill the cup up fully.
  2. Pour coke into the same cup.
  3. Put whipped cream on top for a sweeter flavor and decoration.
