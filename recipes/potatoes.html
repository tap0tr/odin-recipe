Recipe Description

This is a very simple crispy potatoes recipe that you can make with your air fryer at home. This is especially great for those who have a busy schedule and still want to enjoy food.

This takes less than 20 mins that I pair up with any of my favorite source of protein. You'll want to make this every time!

Ingredients

  • Medley potatoes
  • Cooking oil, preferably Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Cajun seasoning, for a bit of spice
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Lemon wedge, for serving
  • Freshly chopped parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in half, bite-sized
  2. In a large bowl, toss potatoes with oil and seasonings. Don't forget salt and pepper!
  3. Place potatoes in air fryer and cook at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Shake basket and cook for another 8 minutes.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over cooked potatoes and garnish with parsley for serving.
